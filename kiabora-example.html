A little example of Kiabora's capabilities.

Input file:

p(X,Z) :- p(X,Y), p(Y,Z).
p(Y,X), q(X,Y,X) :- p(X,Y).
q(X,Z,T) :- r(X,Y).
r(Z,T) :- q(X,Y,Z).
s(Z,Z,Z) :- p(X,X), r(Y,U), s(T,Y,X).
s(Z,X,Y) :- s(X,X,X), s(Y,Y,Y).
